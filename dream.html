About Us

We are a charity organization dedicated to providing care and support for motherless babies.

Our mission is to ensure that every child receives the love, care, and attention they deserve, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Services

  • 24/7 Care and Supervision
  • Healthcare Services
  • Education and Development Programs
  • Family Placement Services
